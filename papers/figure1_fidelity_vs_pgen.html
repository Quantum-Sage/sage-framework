Figure 1. End-to-end fidelity under the stochastic Sage Bound (Theorem 3) as a function of entanglement generation probability pgen, for N = 10 repeater hops across four hardware platforms. The red dashed line marks the Sage Constant S = 0.851 — the minimum fidelity for secure quantum identity persistence. No platform achieves the threshold at any physically realized pgen value. The gray dashed curve shows the deterministic limit (pgen → ∞); at current Willow hardware (pgen = 0.10, left marker) the stochastic bound is 20.7× below the deterministic prediction. The right marker at pgen = 0.47 marks the critical generation probability required for S = 0.851 to become feasible — a 4.7× improvement over current capabilities.
