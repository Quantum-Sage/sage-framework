Figure 3. Stochastic Sage Bound end-to-end fidelity (Theorem 3) for all five intercontinental routes and four hardware platforms. All 20 cells are infeasible: no fidelity value reaches the Sage Constant S = 0.851. The Beijing–London row (red border) is the primary architecture target at 8,200 km. Color depth reflects relative fidelity within the infeasible range; deeper red indicates lower fidelity. The NISQ platform achieves the highest short-route value (0.054) due to its higher pgen = 0.15, despite its lower gate fidelity (0.9500). Helios values are nearly route-independent because the Helios stochastic penalty (1 + 2/0.05 = 41) dominates all other contributions.
